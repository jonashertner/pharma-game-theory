## 1. Situation in one paragraph

Mid-2026 finds Roche embedded in a structured, multi-issue negotiation it cannot exit. Genentech signed an MFN deal in December 2025 covering 17 of 17 major manufacturers (86% of the US branded-drug market). The November 2025 US–Swiss framework capped pharma tariffs at 15% with a 0% Section-232 track through January 2029 for firms building US capacity. Roche has pledged $50B in US capex over 5 years. IRA Round 3 (selected January 2026) placed Xolair under price negotiation with effect January 2028. The current modeled scenario is *Swiss diplomatic carve-out*: Strong Swiss-specific protections: 0% Section 232, deep carve-out language, ex-US firewall reinforced. US gives up some MFN coverage on Roche in exchange for Swiss investment + jobs commitments.
