<h1>Roche &mdash; the durability fight</h1>
<div class="onepager-sub">20 May 2026 &middot; confidential to the board &middot; one page</div>
</div>

<div class="onepager-apex" markdown="1">

The pricing fight is settled. The durability fight has begun. Three Roche-specific exposures &mdash; reference-pricing contagion, the trontinemab launch architecture, and the strategic purpose of the $50B US capex &mdash; carry **two to five times the NPV of every remaining MFN/IRA debate combined**, and are decided in 2026.

</div>

<div class="onepager-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="onepager-block" markdown="1">

### Where we stand

The 19 December 2025 MFN signing landed a stronger Roche outcome than peers: **0% Section&nbsp;232** through January 2029, only Xofluza ceded, the five US growth drivers (Ocrevus, Hemlibra, Vabysmo, Xolair, Phesgo &mdash; **CHF&nbsp;21.4B FY25**) kept out of MFN scope, only Xolair on IRA Round&nbsp;3. The $50B US capex is underway. Pharma Division FY25: **CHF&nbsp;47.7B (+9% CER)**; US 53%; top-5 biologics concentrate 60% of US Pharma revenue.

</div>

<div class="onepager-block" markdown="1">

### What we will do

1. **Endorse the firewall annex** with 60-day pre-conditions. Protect **CHF&nbsp;18&ndash;55B** of 2027&ndash;2031 ex-US revenue against reference-pricing contagion. Decision: this board.
2. **Add trontinemab launch architecture** to the Q3&nbsp;2026 board agenda. NPV at stake: **CHF&nbsp;15&ndash;35B**. The decision is launch sequence and pricing posture, not science.
3. **Re-segment the $50B US capex** into tariff-defense vs franchise-growth tranches; reserve **20% small-molecule-convertible**. NPV at stake: **CHF&nbsp;4&ndash;12B**, with positive skew if Section&nbsp;232 is renewed past 2029.

</div>

<div class="onepager-block" markdown="1">

### What we are watching

- **Reference-pricing spillover or EU forced disclosure** &mdash; tail CHF&nbsp;35&ndash;55B. Watch: AMNOG decisions through Q4&nbsp;2027; EU pharmaceutical strategy implementation milestone Q3&nbsp;2026.
- **Trontinemab Phase&nbsp;3 platform safety** &mdash; probability 10&ndash;20%; CHF&nbsp;25&ndash;40B platform write-down. Watch: TRONTIER 1/2 interim safety reviews; comparable brain-shuttle readouts.
- **2029 Section&nbsp;232 sunset** without renewal &mdash; probability 30&ndash;40%; CHF&nbsp;4&ndash;10B stranded capex. Watch: 2026 midterms; 2028 primary structure; USTR personnel.

</div>

<div class="onepager-block" markdown="1">

### When we revisit

- **30&nbsp;June 2026** &mdash; firewall-annex pre-conditions resolved (USTR sounding, peer-firm peer-test, GC MRA-transfer memo).
- **30&nbsp;September 2026** &mdash; annex text status; trontinemab launch architecture decision frame.
- **Q4&nbsp;2026** &mdash; $50B capex re-segmentation report.
- **20&nbsp;January 2029** &mdash; Section&nbsp;232 sunset decision; if unrenewed, the strategic posture inverts and this memo is re-written.

If any *two* of the named tests resolve against the framework, the strategic posture should be revisited. If all *three*, the framework is wrong.
