How to read this matrix

12 × 13 grid · weights are board-relevant intensities

  • Each cell shows that actor's weight (0–10) on that issue. Empty cells mean the actor has no meaningful stake.
  • Red dot ● marks a red line — the actor walks if breached.
  • Yellow i i marks an inferred position (no direct public statement found).
  • Click any cell for the full position text, BATNA, red-line value, and citation.
  • Where to look first: Roche's row (top); the highest-weight column (10/10) per actor; clusters of red dots indicate brittle issues.
